The Principles

The purpose of this content is to give a brief description of the different Principles.

What are Principles?

Principles are different driving forces of reality. They are fundamental to existence in a similar fashion as death and life.

Why Do They Matter?

The significance of Principles is immediately relevant in occult lore and practices. If one doesn't understand the principles, then meddling in occultism will be futile and dangerous. Knowledge of them will inform both occult practices and foreworn practitioners of their dangers.

Lantern



The Principle of Lantern is often mistaken as a benevolent force. However, the truth is that Lantern reflects illumination. It can be cold and indifferent to those approaching it.
Lantern is associated with the following traits and ideas
  • Knowledge
  • Harshness
  • The Sun
  • The Hours (gods) that come from light

Moth



The Principle of Moth delves into many traits of the world. It is closely associated with nature, both the land and the things within it.
Moth is associated with the following traits and ideas
  • Change
  • Secrecy
  • Unreason
  • Whimsy

Knock



The Principle of Knock is vital to the more mystical acts of occultism. It is used to create links between our world and the other.
Knock is associated with the following traits and ideas
  • Wounds, either of the body, mind, or world
  • Locks
  • Burglars
  • Oracles
  • Entryways

Heart



The Principle of Heart is a rather optimistic force in our world. It symbolizes a constant effort to continue existing, which makes the Principle valued by those on death's door.
Heart is associated with the following traits and ideas
  • Life
  • The dance that must never cease
  • Preservation
  • Union
  • Protection
